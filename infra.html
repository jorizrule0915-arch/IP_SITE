Execution Model

A Disciplined Infrastructure Process

Structured methodology from origination through full lifecycle governance.

Phase01

Origination

Preliminary project review and eligibility assessment against underwriting criteria, sector focus, and structural viability benchmarks.

Phase02

Institutional Underwriting

Revenue durability modeling and stress testing across all risk categories. Counterparty strength, asset essentiality, and regulatory stability assessment.

Phase03

Capital Structuring

SPV formation, capital stack definition, covenant drafting, revenue waterfall design, and reserve structure determination. Governance documentation established.

Phase04

Deployment Oversight

Construction milestone monitoring, compliance verification against covenant thresholds, and reserve adequacy tracking through the full deployment period.

Phase05

Lifecycle Governance

Quarterly reporting cadence, reserve position monitoring, four-tier escalation protocol activation when thresholds are triggered, and independent audit coordination.

Governance Escalation

Four-Tier Escalation Framework

Level 01

Reporting Alert

Covenant threshold proximity or reporting variance triggers formal notification and increased monitoring cadence.

Level 02

Covenant Review

Formal review with structured response timeline, stakeholder notification, and compliance certification requirement.

Level 03

Remedial Action Plan

Mandatory RAP with defined milestones, reserve review, increased reporting frequency, and board update.

Level 04

Structural Intervention

If contractually defined, structural intervention mechanisms activated per the SPV governance documentation.
